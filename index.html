Project Summary

We explore node selection in extremely low-resource settings (i.e. low vaccine availability) using heuristic immunisation algorithms. We run a modeling study to show that it is more effective to vaccinate bridge nodes rather than vaccinate high-degree nodes. We compare seven selection methods under resource-constrained conditions. We run SIR and Covasim models and compare over existing population and synthetic contact networks data. We display our key results below.
